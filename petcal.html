PetCal App.

A helpful app when feeding my dogs.

I wanted to improve the nutrition of my own dogs by rotating different kibble brands and protein sources. Every brand or even model has different calorie counts and my boy needs to lose weight!

Every time we introduce a new food, we add it gradually so it took a lot of math every 2 days, using calculators, spreadsheets and Discord for sharing, which was very cumbersome...

How it works

I determined my dogs' energy requirements. There are tools online for that and the plan is to add it in the app too! I used the food calorie count and decided on the number of meals I wanted to feed per day.

If I'm introducing a new food, I add the calories written on the package and percentage I want to use. The new food percentage is increased every 2 days.

The numbers displayed in the app represent amounts per meal. Also, the device doesn’t go to sleep while the numbers are in view, so I have plenty of time to measure and feed!
